Who We Are

Rooted in Purpose,
Driven by Compassion

Pratap Chandra Das Memorial Trust is a charitable organisation dedicated to empowering India's most vulnerable communities through compassionate, transparent, and sustainable programs that create lasting, meaningful change.

Our Story

How It All Began

Born from the vision of one extraordinary individual, this trust was founded with a single conviction: organised compassion can change the world.

Est.
2025
Year Founded

In 2025, Pratap Chandra Das Memorial Trust was established in the memory and honour of its founder — a man who spent his life serving those around him, often at great personal sacrifice. His passing left a void, but it also left behind a legacy so powerful that those who loved him resolved to carry it forward.

The trust was born not from a boardroom but from the heart of a community — shaped by the people who witnessed first-hand the suffering that went unaddressed, and who knew that inaction in the face of need is itself a choice.

Starting with a commitment to serve the hungry, the elderly, and the injured, PCDMT has grown into a structured, transparent, and impactful organisation. Our programs are direct, our methods are honest, and our purpose is singular: to serve with dignity, every single time.

Today we serve communities across our region with three core programs — delivering meals, honouring elders, and extending emergency medical aid — all sustained by the generous hearts of our donors and the tireless dedication of our volunteers.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Commitment

The three pillars that guide everything we do — from the decisions we make to the communities we serve, day after day.

🎯

Our Mission

To serve the most vulnerable members of our community — the hungry, the elderly, the sick, and the injured — with dignity, compassion, and reliability. We exist to ensure that no one faces hardship alone.

🌟

Our Vision

A compassionate India where every person — regardless of their circumstance — receives the care and dignity they deserve. We envision communities where no one is forgotten, no call for help goes unanswered.

🤝

Our Commitment

We operate with radical transparency and community-first thinking. Every rupee donated is accounted for, every act of service is documented, and every donor receives confirmation of the impact they create.

Accountability & Trust

How We Work

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of transparency and accountability. Every donor, every volunteer, and every community member deserves to know exactly how trust is built and maintained.

📸

Real-Time Documentation

Every service event — every meal, every elder visit, every emergency response — is photographed and documented. We maintain a live record of our activities and share updates with donors directly on WhatsApp.

💳

Direct Donation Receipts

Every donation, no matter how small, receives an acknowledgement. We send a personalised WhatsApp receipt with proof of impact within minutes of receiving your contribution, so you always know your money matters.

🤝

Community Accountability

Our programs are shaped and reviewed by the communities we serve. Local feedback drives improvements, and community members are involved in our planning, ensuring that our work stays relevant, respectful, and real.

📅

Consistent, Predictable Service

We show up — every 6th and 26th, every month, without fail. Consistency builds trust, and trust is the foundation of everything we do. Communities know when to expect us, and we never let them down.

🌐

Open Social Presence

Follow our work live on Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and WhatsApp Channel. We document and share every program openly so anyone can see — in real time — the difference their support makes.

❤️

Zero Overhead Policy

Every rupee donated goes directly to program delivery. Our operational costs are covered by committed core supporters, ensuring that when you donate for a meal, that donation feeds someone — fully and completely.
