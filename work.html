Our Programs

What We Do

Each initiative is rooted in compassion and shaped by the real needs of the communities we serve — from warm meals to medical care, warm clothing to life-saving aid.

Food Aid

Anand Arna Seba

Our flagship food relief program — meaning "Service of the House of Joy" — distributes cooked meals and dry rations to the hungry on the 6th and 26th of every month. Every gathering is as much a community celebration as it is a meal.

Sanman Seba

Sanman Seba — meaning "Service with Honour" — uplifts the dignity of marginalised individuals and families by recognising their contributions and meeting their immediate welfare needs with respect and compassion.

Healthcare

Jiban Rakhshya Seba

Jiban Rakhshya Seba — meaning "Life Protection Service" — organises free medical camps, distributes medicines, and provides emergency health support to underserved communities far from the reach of hospitals.
